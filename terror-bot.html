Terror-Bot is an intelligent defense robot capable of real-time human classification and surveillance in high-risk zones. Featuring a custom face-detection model with 92% accuracy, the system distinguishes between soldiers and threats using a Python-based detection module. Wireless control via Arduino and embedded C reduced latency by 35%, while optimized firmware improved operational efficiency by 40%. The bot supports stable live video streaming (98% uptime) and was recognized by the Karnataka State Council for Science and Technology in 2023, placing among the top 22.8% of innovative projects in threat detection.
